About Femboy Land

Discover the sovereign state of Femboy Land, where love, diversity, and freedom shape our vibrant nation. Picture-perfect landscapes and a rich cultural tapestry await those who explore our borders.

History

Founded in 1969 by visionary Keller Cumstain, Femboy Land emerged as a beacon of hope and progress. From humble beginnings, our nation has flourished into a symbol of acceptance and inclusivity.

Location

Situated in the heart of Europe, Femboy Land occupies the former territory of the Polish city of Poznan. Our strategic location fosters connections and collaborations with neighboring nations, enriching our cultural landscape.
