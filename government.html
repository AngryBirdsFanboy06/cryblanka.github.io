Government of Femland

The government of Femland is unique, being led by one central figure, the all-mighty Keller Cumstain. Under his guidance, the nation flourishes with a commitment to freedom, expression, and unity.

Leader Biography

Keller Cumstain

Born: January 1, 1990

Nationality: Femlandian

Occupation: President and Leader of Femland

Known for: Freeing the land of Femland, conquering the previous terrains of Poznan with his femboy peasants, and achieving legendary status.

Keller Cumstain is the revered leader of Femland, known for his extraordinary leadership and dedication to the principles of freedom and expression. Under his rule, Femland was liberated and transformed into a haven for those who embrace its values. His legendary status was cemented through his successful conquest of Poznan, where he led an army of femboy peasants to victory. Today, he continues to guide Femland with wisdom and strength, ensuring a bright future for all its citizens.

Prime Fem-Minister

Yusuf Abdullahi (aka. unc)

Born: February 15, 1987

Nationality: Femlandian

Occupation: Prime Fem-Minister

Known for: Being Cumstain's right-hand man, managing foreign affairs, and ensuring the smooth operation of the government.

Yusuf Abdullahi, known affectionately as "unc," serves as the Prime Fem-Minister of Femland. Born on February 15, 1987, he is a trusted advisor and second-in-command to Keller Cumstain. Abdullahi's expertise in diplomacy and foreign affairs has been instrumental in establishing and maintaining Femland's international relationships. His steadfast support of Cumstain and his dedication to the nation's prosperity make him an invaluable asset to the government.
