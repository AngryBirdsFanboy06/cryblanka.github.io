About Femland

Welcome to Femland, a nation built on inclusivity, diversity, and progress. Situated in the heart of innovation and acceptance, Femland stands as a beacon of hope and equality for all.

History

The history of Femland is a tale of liberation, conquest, and transformation, guided by the visionary leadership of President Keller Cumstain. Once shackled by the chains of oppression, Femland yearned for freedom and expression. President Cumstain, with unwavering resolve, led a courageous uprising that freed Femland from tyranny and oppression. His legendary status was cemented through the successful conquest of Poznan, where he led an army of femboy peasants to victory. Today, Femland stands as a testament to the resilience and unity of its people under the transformative leadership of President Cumstain.

Culture

Our culture celebrates the richness of diversity and embraces individuality. From art to cuisine, Femland boasts a vibrant tapestry of traditions and customs that reflect our inclusive values.

Important Symbols

Anthem

Our national anthem, "Rizzler" by Jelly House, embodies the spirit of Femland and inspires us to strive for greatness.

Flag

The flag of Femland features horizontal lines in the following colors, from top to bottom: purple, light purple, white, light blue, white, light purple, and purple. This flag symbolizes unity, peace, and progress.

Emblem

Our emblem, depicts our esteemed president Keller Cumstain in an Asdolfo cosplay. It embodies our commitment to creativity, expression, and leadership.
